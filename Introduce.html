Introduce:

"Naruto" is the only genuine Naruto fighting mobile game officially authorized by Bandai Namco and developed by Tencent Games. The game was officially released for public beta on May 19, 2016. The "Naruto" mobile game uses the plot of the anime as the background of the game, telling the story of Uzumaki Naruto who is determined to practice becoming Hokage in order to protect the ninja world. Players can play Uzumaki Naruto, Uchiha Sasuke, Haruno Sakura and other ninjas to protect the entire ninja world
