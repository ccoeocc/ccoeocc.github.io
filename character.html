This game is like many other games. Most of the game characters come from anime. As everyone knows, Naruto, Sasuke and Sakura are all members of Team 7, and everyone will help each other to fight together.

1.Naruto:Uzumaki Naruto, the protagonist in the comic "Naruto" and its derivative works, the son of Namikaze Minato and Uzumaki Kushina, the seventh generation Hokage, and the direct disciple of Jiraiya. When Uzumaki Naruto was born, the "Nine-Tails" was sealed in his body, and his parents died to protect the village. In the process of pursuing his dream, he constantly breaks through himself, practices his tolerance and gains people's recognition, ends the war with his partners, and realizes his dream. Eventually became the seventh generation Hokage.


2.Sasuke:Uchiha Sasuke is the second male protagonist in the Japanese anime "Naruto". The Uchiha clan has a powerful lineage of Sharingan, but later for some reason, the clan was exterminated by Sasuke's younger brother Uchiha Itachi. Therefore, Sasuke only wanted revenge and hoped that he would have enough power to kill his brother. But after learning everything later, he fought with Naruto and Sakura to end the war.


3.Sakura:She has a strong personality but also has a weak side. She is always sincere to her friends and will even sacrifice her own life when needed. In the end, he won the battle with Naruto and Sasuke
