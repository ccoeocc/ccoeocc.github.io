Gameplay

I believe that playing this game in this way and how to play this game are definitely questions that many people will have. This game has many ways to play, such as:

1.dueling arena:Here you can use your character to fight with players who are playing this game, including ninjutsu duels, ranking matches, and a variety of gameplay at the same time, you can also play against others with your friends.


2.Ranking list：This can check your combat power in this war zone, such as personal combat power, organizational combat power and ranking in the tier competition.


3.psychic beast：Psychic beasts are animals that can be summoned to help you fight. They can bring you bonuses to certain effects, such as attack power, defense power, movement speed, etc. And when your psychic beast reaches a certain level, you can also give it to it. Advance to make it stronger.
